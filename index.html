  • Dissertation

    • Title :’实测地震地面和楼层加速度数据及对结构影响的分析-以近期宜宾地震实测数据为对象（Analysis of Ground and Floor Acceleration Measured Data and Structural Effect - Taking Yibing earthquake measured data as research object）’

    • Content

      Keywords–Time-Frequency Analysis, Signal Processing, Fourier Transform, Response Spectrum, Matlab, Python

      • Background

        1. The earthquakes happened frequently in Yibin of Sichuan Province, the largest magnitude of which reaches 6. Besides, the epicenters are located intensively between two counties of Yibin, an area of nearly 120 km^2. The seismic precautionary intensity and design standard should be considered to improve for safety risks. With the help of Bureau of Housing and Urban-Rural Development and Department of earthquake prevention and disaster mitigation, there are two sets of self-developed IoT intelligent hardware separately installed in a seismic station and a six-floor brick house in Gongxian First High School. The thesis then analysed signal data collected from the hardware during one month when there are five valid earthquake signals supervised.’

      • Methodology

        1. Signal preprocessing :’detrend using least squares polynomial fitting, and do smoothing processing using moving average.’
        2. Filtering :’filter the high-frequency part through discrete Fourier transform.
        3. Acceleration integral :’ integrated acceleration and obtained the relative velocity and displacement.’
        4. Statistical data :’mean value, mean square error, variance, autocorrelation and cross-correlation.’
        5. Frequency information :’Fourier amplitude spectrum and phase spectrum,PSD (power spectra density) to figure out the structural natural frequency, FRF (frequency response function), coherency function.’
        6. Response spectrum :’Newmark-β linear acceleration method to get acceleration, relative velocity, relative displacement response spectra and acceleration dynamic amplification factor curve; compared with design response spectrum in building seismic design standard.’
        7. Structural dynamic response analysis :’modeling, doing elastic time-history response analysis using PKPM to compare acceleration response spectrum and design response spectrum to verify the local seismic design standard should be improved.’
        8. Earthquake history and PSHA (Probabilistic Seismic Hazard Analysis) :’collected more than 100 earthquake records ever since 1900, applied Weibull distribution model fitting intensity probability distribution which confromed to exponweib through Kolmogorov-Smirnov test. According to the model the seismic precautionary intensity is 7.2 well above the prescribed standard intensity 5.8.’

      • Conclusion

        1. natural frequency of structure
        2. comparison between response spectrum and design spectrum
        3. The value of dynamic amplification factor is bigger than standard value
        4. The earthquake frequency exceeds the seismic fortification level. The seismic fortification intensity should be increased.
